How does gravity work?

The answer is gravity: an invisible force that pulls objects toward each other. ... So, the closer objects are to each other,
the stronger their gravitational pull is. Earth's gravity comes from all its mass. All its mass makes a combined gravitational pull on all the mass in your body.

What is gravity exactly? Gravity is a force of attraction that exists between any two masses, any two bodies, any two particles. Gravity is not just the attraction between objects and the Earth. It is an attraction that exists between all objects, everywhere in the universe.
